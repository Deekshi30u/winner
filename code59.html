Major Events

  • Youth Red Cross Wing (YRCW) Day

    The VV chapter and Youth Red Cross Wing of P.E.S College of Engineering, Mandya, jointly organized the "VV & Youth Red Cross Day" on November 4th, 2022, in MBA Smart Classroom-2. This annual event aimed to recognize outstanding student volunteers within the ISTE & YRCW of the college, celebrating their dedication and achievements throughout the academic year. Dr. R M Mahalinge Gowda, the Principal of P.E.S College of Engineering, Mandya, and head of the Youth Red Cross Wing, graced the occasion as the chief guest. The former Chairman of the ISTE Faculty Chapter, Dr. B S Shivakumara, along with Dr. M N Veena, Chairman of the VV Faculty chapter, Dr. B Shanmukha, the Programme officer of YRCW, and Dr. Nayaka S R, Secretary/Treasurer of YRCW, were also present. The event began at 4:00 pm with the lighting of the lamp by the Principal and the executive officers of VV & YRCW. Notably, all 66 VV faculty members were invited, and the executive committee members of the vv Faculty chapter were present. Student winners of various competitions were honored with cash prizes and certificates, with VV Student Chapter members also in attendance. Dr. M N Veena welcomed everyone, extending warm greetings. Dr. Nayaka S R highlighted the VV PESCE Chapter's achievements, including the Best Faculty Chapter Awards in 2021 and 2022 and the Best Chapter Award secured by the VV PESCE Student Chapter in 2016. He also detailed various events conducted by VV throughout the year. Dr. B Shanmukha discussed YRCW's initiatives, such as blood donation drives and social activities like Street Dog Feeding and Trekking organized during the academic year 2021-22.

a luminary in technology, business, and social impact, is introduced in the Orientation Program of PES College of Engineering.
