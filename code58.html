Club Goals

Our Club goal is providing quality training programmes to teachers and administrators of technical institutions to update their knowledge and skills in their fields of activity.

Club Description

The Future Innovators Society is a vibrant community of technophiles, innovators, and problem-solvers dedicated to exploring the cutting edge of technology and pushing the boundaries of what's possible. Our club serves as a hub for students passionate about all things technical, from software development and robotics to artificial intelligence and beyond. Mission: Our mission is to foster creativity, collaboration, and hands-on learning in the field of technology. We aim to provide a platform where members can exchange ideas, develop their skills, and work on exciting projects that have real-world impact. Activities: Workshops and Tech Talks: We organize regular workshops and tech talks led by industry professionals and knowledgeable club members. Topics range from programming languages and software tools to emerging technologies and industry trends. Project-Based Learning: Members have the opportunity to participate in various projects spanning different domains of technology. Whether it's building a mobile app, designing a robot, or creating an AI-powered solution, there's something for everyone to sink their teeth into. Hackathons and Competitions: We host hackathons and participate in competitions to challenge our members and put their skills to the test. These events provide a platform for innovation, teamwork, and rapid prototyping. Networking Events: We organize networking events where members can connect with like-minded individuals, potential mentors, and industry professionals. Building a strong network is crucial for personal and professional growth in the tech world. Membership: Membership in the Future Innovators Society is open to all students with a passion for technology, regardless of their academic background or level of expertise. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out on your tech journey, there's a place for you in our community. Join Us: If you're ready to dive into the exciting world of technology and be part of a supportive community of innovators, join the Future Innovators Society today! Keep an eye out for our events and meetings, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions.

Open MIC

The P.E.S College of Engineering in Mandya hosted the engaging OPEN MIC 4.0 on June 19, 2023, at the CRC Amphitheatre. Organized by VV, the event provided a platform for 35 talented students to showcase their musical abilities across diverse genres, including solo film, classical, instrumental, and Bhavageethe performances, alongside 10 impressive group film song renditions. With esteemed judges like D M Srinivas, S.K Uma, and Archana G, and support from Dr. Nayaka S.R and Dr. M N Veena, the event commenced at 4:15 pm, concluding at 7:20 pm, followed by an efficient clean-up by 7:45 pm. This successful program not only celebrated musical talent but also provided valuable learning experiences in leadership, time management, and event organization for the many enthusiastic volunteers involved...

The Forgotten Library

The library stood at the edge of the ancient forest, its stone walls weathered by time and its windows obscured by creeping ivy. Within its dimly lit halls, shelves sagged under the weight of forgotten tomes, their spines cracked and pages yellowed with age. It was a place where the whispers of history echoed through the shadows, a sanctuary for knowledge lost to the passage of time. Evelyn Rivers had always been drawn to the library, ever since she was a child. Growing up in the nearby village of Eldoria, she would spend hours exploring its labyrinthine corridors, losing herself in the tales of distant lands and forgotten legends. Now, as a young woman on the cusp of adulthood, she found herself drawn back to its hallowed halls once more.
Pushing open the heavy oak door, Evelyn stepped into the library, her footsteps echoing against the stone floor. Dust motes danced in the slivers of sunlight that streamed through the windows, casting a warm glow over the rows of books that lined the shelves. She took a deep breath, inhaling the familiar scent of parchment and ink that hung in the air. As Evelyn wandered deeper into the library, her fingers trailed along the spines of the books, each one a doorway to another world waiting to be discovered. She paused before a shelf marked "Ancient Tomes," her gaze lingering on a particularly weathered volume nestled among its companions. With a sense of anticipation fluttering in her chest, she reached out and plucked the book from its resting place. The cover was adorned with intricate runes, their meaning lost to all but the most learned scholars. Running her fingers over the embossed symbols, Evelyn felt a thrill of excitement course through her veins. This was no ordinary book; it was a relic of a bygone era, a window into the mysteries of the past. With trembling hands, Evelyn opened the book and began to read, her eyes devouring the words that danced across the pages. As she delved deeper into its pages, she felt a sense of connection to something greater than herself, as if she were touching the very fabric of history itself. Hours passed in a blur as Evelyn lost herself in the pages of the ancient tome, her mind alight with wonder and curiosity. But as the sun dipped below the horizon and the shadows lengthened in the library, she knew that it was time to return to the world outside. Closing the book with a sense of reluctance, Evelyn tucked it under her arm and made her way back to the entrance of the library. As she stepped out into the cool evening air, she felt a newfound sense of purpose stirring within her. Little did she know that her journey was only just beginning, and that the secrets she had uncovered within the pages of the ancient tome would set her on a path that would change the course of history forever.
